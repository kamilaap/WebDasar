Recipe of the Day

Featured Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix flour and baking powder in a bowl.
  3. Beat eggs and sugar until fluffy.
  4. Add butter and vanilla extract, mix well.
  5. Combine with dry ingredients, stir until smooth.
  6. Pour batter into a greased baking dish.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until golden brown.
